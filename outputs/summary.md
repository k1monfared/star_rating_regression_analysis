## Caveats

- Synthetic data only. The purpose is to demonstrate the analysis type and communication, not to report real platform metrics.
- Observational design. Star rating is not randomly assigned, so the recovered relationships are associations after adjusting for observed confounders. Unobserved confounders could remain.
- A causal estimate would need randomization (for example randomizing a nudge in the composer) or a credible natural experiment.
